# User Manuals

---

# 1. District User Manual

## Login & Dashboard

1. Open the application URL and log in with your credentials.
2. You land on the **Collector's Dashboard** — shows summary of all blocks, schools, students, test results, and attendance in your district.

> If prompted to change your password, do so immediately — access is blocked until you comply.

---

## Navigation Menu

| Menu Item | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Dashboard | District-wide overview and charts |
| Manage Blocks | Create/edit blocks |
| Manage Schools | Create/edit schools across all blocks |
| Academic Calendar | View and manage district events |
| Activity Logs | Full audit trail of all actions |

---

## Managing Blocks

1. Click **Manage Blocks** → **Add Block**.
2. Enter name (English & Hindi), assign a block admin user, and save.
3. Use **Edit** to update, **Toggle** to activate/deactivate.

---

## Managing Schools

1. Click **Manage Schools** → **Add School**.
2. Enter name, NIC code, select block, assign school admin, and save.
3. Edit or toggle schools the same way as blocks.

---

## Reports & Analytics

- **Student Ranking** — cumulative or test-wise
- **Top / Weakest Students** — filter by test
- **Schools without Students / Inactive Schools**
- **Test-wise Average Marks** — cross-school performance
- **Test Results Analysis** — score distribution charts

---

## Attendance

- **Submit Attendance** — daily entry
- **Attendance Summary** — district-wide overview
- Block-wise, date-wise, and school-wise summaries available from the Attendance menu

---

## Academic Calendar

1. Go to **Academic Calendar → Manage** → **Add Event**.
2. Enter title, date, type (Exam, Holiday, Meeting, etc.) and save.
3. Edit or delete events using the buttons next to each entry.

---

## Bulk Data Upload

- **Upload School Users** — import users via Excel (download sample template first)
- **Upload Student Data** — bulk import student records

---

## Activity Logs

- Go to **Activity Logs** to see all district actions.
- Filter by **date range** or **action type**.
- Each entry shows: timestamp, action, user, description, IP address.

---

## Tests

- Activate or deactivate tests from the **Tests** section.
- View results per test or across all tests.

---

## Important Notes

- You can only see data **within your own district**.
- Account locks after repeated failed login attempts.
- Password expires every **90 days**.
- All actions are recorded in the Activity Log.

---

*For issues, contact your State Administrator.*

# 2. School User Manual

## Login & Dashboard

1. Open the application URL and log in with your credentials.
2. You land on the **School Dashboard** — shows your school's test statistics, student performance, and mark distributions.

> If prompted to change your password, do so immediately.

---

## Navigation Menu

| Menu Item | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Dashboard | School performance overview |
| Question Paper Generator | Create AI-generated question papers |
| Academic Calendar | View district calendar events |
| Logout | End your session |

---

## Managing Students

1. Go to **Students** from the dashboard.
2. **Add Student** — enter student details and save.
3. **Edit** — update student information.
4. **Delete** — remove a student record.
5. **Upload Student Data** — bulk import from Excel.

> You can only manage students in **your own school**.

---

## Entering Marks

1. Go to **Marks** or open an active test from the dashboard.
2. Select the test, enter marks for each student, and save.
3. Edit or delete individual mark entries as needed.
4. Only marks for students in your school are visible.

---

## Attendance

1. Go to **Attendance → Submit Attendance**.
2. Select the absent students for the day and submit.
3. View summaries under **Attendance Summary** (date-wise, present/absent counts, percentages).

---

## Reports & Analytics

- **School Average Marks** — average per test and overall percentage for your school
- **Student Ranking** — rank students by cumulative or test-wise performance
- **Top Students** — students with full marks (filter by test)
- **Weakest Students** — filter by test and score threshold
- **Analysis Dashboard** — select an individual student to view their full test history
- **Test Results Analysis** — score distribution charts for your school

---

## Active Tests

- **Active Test List** — view all currently active tests in your district.
- Click a test to enter marks for your students.
- Activate or deactivate tests as needed.

---

## Question Paper Generator

1. Go to **Question Paper Generator**.
2. Select subject, topic, question types (MCQ, True/False, Short Answer, etc.).
3. Choose language (English / Hindi) and generate.
4. Download or print the generated paper.

---

## Academic Calendar

- Go to **Academic Calendar** to view district-wide events.
- Events include: teaching sessions, exams, holidays, meetings.
- School users can **view only** — calendar events are managed by the District user.

---

## Important Notes

- You can only see and manage data for **your own school**.
- Password expires every **90 days** — change it before expiry.
- Account locks after repeated failed login attempts.
- All your actions are tracked in the system audit log.

---

*For issues, contact your Block or District Administrator.*

# 3. Student User Manual

## Login

1. Open the application URL and go to the **Student Login** page.
2. Enter your **Roll Number** and **Password**.
3. Solve the **math CAPTCHA** and click Login.

> After 5 failed attempts your account is locked for 30 minutes. Contact your school if you forget your password.

---

## Dashboard

After login you land on your **Dashboard**, which shows:

| Card | What it shows |
|---|---|
| Tests Completed | Total number of tests attempted |
| Average Score | Your overall average percentage |
| Highest Score | Your best test percentage |
| Attendance | Your attendance percentage |

Below the cards you can see your **Recent Test Results** — test name, subject, marks, and date.

---

## Quick Actions

From the dashboard you can access:

| Action | What you can do |
|---|---|
| **View Tests** | See all active and completed tests |
| **Performance** | Detailed subject-wise and trend analysis |
| **Practice Test** | Take topic-based practice tests |
| **AI Sathi** | Ask doubts and get help |
| **Recommendations** | Get study tips based on your weak areas |
| **Video Learning** | Watch educational videos |
| **Change Password** | Update your login password |

---

## My Tests

1. Click **View Tests** from the dashboard.
2. See the list of active and completed tests.
3. Click any test to view:
   - Marks obtained and maximum marks
   - Your percentage
   - Question paper (if available for download)

---

## Performance

1. Click **Performance** from the dashboard.
2. View your:
   - **Subject-wise averages** — which subjects you are strong/weak in
   - **Test-wise trend** — your last 10 test scores
   - **Grade distribution** — how many A+, A, B, C, D grades you have earned

---

## Practice Tests

1. Click **Practice Test** from the dashboard.
2. Select a topic from your curriculum.
3. Attempt the practice questions.
4. View your score and track your improvement over time under **Practice Progress**.

---

## Attendance

Your attendance percentage is shown on the dashboard. It is updated by your school — you cannot edit it yourself.

---

## Change Password

1. Click **Change Password** from the dashboard.
2. Enter your current password, then your new password twice.
3. Click Save.

---

## Logout

Always click **Logout** when you finish to keep your account secure.

---

## Important Notes

- You can only see your **own** marks and data — not other students'.
- You **cannot edit** marks, attendance, or any records — contact your school for corrections.
- Your tests are from your **district only**.
- All logins and logouts are recorded by the system.

---

*For login issues or password reset, contact your school administrator.*
