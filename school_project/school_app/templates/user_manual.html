Login & Getting Started

All Users

PadhaiWithAI has two separate login portals — one for staff (School, Block, District, State) and one for Students.

Staff Login (School / Block / District / State)

Open the login page

Go to the system URL. The login page opens by default.

Enter Email & Password

Type your assigned email address and password in the fields provided.

Complete the CAPTCHA

Solve the security CAPTCHA to confirm you are a real user. Click the refresh icon if the image is unclear.

Click "Login"

You will be redirected to your role-specific dashboard automatically.

Screenshot: Login Page
Login form with Email, Password, CAPTCHA fields and Login button

Student Login

Go to Student Login

Navigate to /student/login/ or click the "Student Login" link on the main login page.

Enter your Roll Number & Password

Use your school-assigned roll number and password. Default password is your roll number (change it after first login).

Select your Class & School

Choose your class from the dropdown to ensure correct data is shown.

Tip: If you forget your password, contact your school admin (for students) or district officer (for school/block users) to reset it.
Important: Do not share your password. Each login is tracked in the Activity Log.

School / Teacher Guide

School Login

After logging in, you land on the School Dashboard — your central hub for managing students, marks, attendance, and AI tools.

Manage StudentsAdd, edit, delete students
Enter MarksTest-wise marks entry
AttendanceDaily attendance marking
Question PaperAI-generated papers
Academic CalendarView district schedule
AI ToolsMath solver & tutor
Adding Students

Go to Students → Add Student

Click "Students" in the navigation, then "Add Student".

Fill in the form

Enter Student Name, Father's Name, Class (6–10), Roll Number, and Date of Birth.

Click Save

The student is added to your school. They will appear in your student list immediately.

Bulk Upload: To add many students at once, use Upload Student Data from the dashboard. Download the sample Excel file, fill it, and upload.
Entering Test Marks

Go to Active Test List

From the dashboard, click "Enter Marks" or navigate to the active test list.

Select a Test

Only tests activated by your District Officer will appear. Click "Enter Marks" next to the test.

Enter marks for each student

A table shows all your students. Type marks in the input field. Marks must be between 0 and the maximum marks.

Save & Submit

Click "Save Marks". You can edit them later if needed before the test is deactivated.

Screenshot: Marks Entry Page
Table with student names in rows and marks input fields

Marking Daily Attendance

Open Attendance

Click "Attendance" in the top navigation menu.

Mark Present / Absent

Each student has a Present / Absent toggle. Mark accordingly for today.

Submit

Click "Submit Attendance". Today's attendance is saved and visible to block and district officers.

Note: Attendance should be marked daily. Missing dates will show as 0% attendance in reports.
Academic Calendar

The Academic Calendar shows your district's teaching schedule, exam dates, and holidays — set by the District Officer.

Click "Academic Calendar" in the navigation

A colour-coded monthly calendar is shown. Use Previous / Next to navigate months.

Read event colours

🔵 Blue = Teaching days  |  🔴 Red = Exam / Assessment  |  🟢 Green = Holiday  |  🟡 Yellow = Meeting

Viewing Your Dashboard

Your dashboard shows: total students, active tests, attendance today, and recent performance charts. Use Quick Action buttons to navigate to common tasks.

Block Officer Guide

Block Login

Block officers monitor all schools within their block. They can view performance, attendance, and manage schools — but cannot create tests.

Block Dashboard Overview

The Block Dashboard shows:

SectionWhat It Shows

Student Performance ChartsDoughnut charts showing how many students fall in each marks category (0–33%, 33–60% etc.) per test
Test-Wise School PerformanceBar charts showing how many schools are in each category per test

Managing Schools

Click "Manage Schools"

View all schools in your block with their status (active/inactive).

Edit or Toggle Status

Edit school details or mark a school as inactive if it has stopped functioning.

Viewing Reports

From Quick Actions, access:

  • School Report — average marks per school per test
  • Student Analysis — top and weakest students in your block
  • Report Dashboard — full analytics overview
  • Academic Calendar — district teaching schedule
All data shown is automatically filtered to only your block — you will never see data from other blocks.
04

District Officer (Collector) Guide

District Login

District officers have the most administrative power at the ground level. They manage schools, create tests, manage the academic calendar, and view district-wide analytics.

Creating a Test

Go to "Schedule New Test"

From the dashboard or navigation, click "Add Test".

Fill in Test Details

Enter Test Name, Subject, Max Marks, Test Date. Optionally upload Question Paper PDF and Answer Key PDF.

Save the Test

The test is created but NOT visible to schools yet.

Activate the Test

From the test list, click "Activate" to make the test visible and allow schools to enter marks.

Deactivate when done

Once all schools have entered marks, click "Deactivate" to close marks entry.

Only Active tests appear in schools' marks entry screen. Deactivated tests are still visible for reporting.
Uploading School Users (Bulk)

Go to Upload Users

Navigate to /upload-users/ from the dashboard.

Download the Sample Excel

Click the green "Download Sample Excel File" button. Open it and fill in your school data.

Fill Required Columns

The file needs: email, username, password, school_name, nic_code, block_id. Get Block IDs from the Manage Blocks page.

Upload the file

Choose your filled Excel file and click "Upload Users". School logins are created instantly.

Screenshot: Upload Users Page
Green download button at top, file upload form below, with Hindi and English instructions

Managing Academic Calendar

Click "Academic Calendar" → "Manage Events"

This page is only available to District Officers.

Add a New Event

Fill in the Event Title, Type (Teaching / Exam / Holiday / Meeting), Start Date, End Date, then click "Add".

View in Calendar Preview

The calendar preview updates immediately. All schools and block officers in your district will see the changes.

Delete an Event

Click the red 🗑 Delete button next to any event in the events list.

Activity Logs

Go to Activity Logs to see a full record of all user logins and actions in your district. Useful for security monitoring and accountability.

State Admin Guide

State Login

State admins have a bird's-eye view of the entire state — all districts, blocks, schools, and students.

State Dashboard

Shows total districts, blocks, schools, students, tests, and active users across the state. Includes:

  • Bar chart — District-wise School & Student distribution

AI Math Solver

School / Teacher

The AI Math Solver solves any mathematics problem and gives a complete step-by-step explanation in Hindi or English.

Go to Math Tools

Click "Math Tools" in the top navigation (available for school login).

Type or upload your problem

Type the math question in the text box. You can also click the camera/image icon to upload a photo of a handwritten problem.

Select language

Choose Hindi or English for the explanation.

Click "Solve"

The AI returns a full solution with numbered steps, formulas (rendered as proper math equations), and an explanation.

Generate similar questions

Click "Generate More" to get practice questions on the same topic.

Works for Class 6–12 Mathematics including: Algebra, Geometry, Trigonometry, Statistics, and more.
08

AI Sathi (Smart Tutor)

Students School

AI Sathi is a smart tutor that answers questions only from your specific subject, class, and chapter — so it stays focused and on-topic.

Click "AI Sathi" in the navigation

The tutor page opens with a chat interface.

Select Class, Subject, and Chapter

The AI will only answer questions related to these selections — this keeps answers curriculum-aligned.

Type your question

Ask in Hindi or English. For example: "त्रिभुज की परिभाषा क्या है?" or "What is the area formula for a triangle?"

Read the answer

The AI responds in the same language with a clear, step-by-step explanation. Click the speaker icon to have it read aloud.

Download as PDF

Click "Download PDF" to save the answer for offline study.

AI Sathi is available 24/7. Students can use it for homework help, exam revision, or understanding difficult concepts.

Frequently Asked Questions

I forgot my password. What do I do?
Students should contact their school teacher. School admins should contact the Block or District Officer. District/Block officers should contact the State Admin to reset passwords.
I cannot see any test in the marks entry screen.
Tests must be activated by the District Officer before schools can enter marks. Contact your District Officer to activate the test.
The AI Math Solver is not giving an answer. What's wrong?
Check your internet connection. If the problem persists, try rephrasing the question. For image uploads, ensure the image is clear and well-lit. The AI may occasionally timeout — refresh and try again.
Why does the block dashboard show no charts?
Charts appear only when there is marks data entered for your block's schools. Ensure schools have submitted marks for at least one active test.
Can I upload students in bulk instead of one by one?
Yes. Go to Upload Student Data from the dashboard. Download the sample Excel file, fill in student details, and upload. All students are created at once.
The Academic Calendar is not showing. What should I do?
Events are added by the District Officer. If no events are added yet, the calendar will be empty. Contact your District Officer to add the academic schedule.
How many questions does AI Sathi answer per session?
There is no fixed limit. You can ask as many questions as needed in a session. Conversation history is maintained within the session.
Why can I see tests from other districts?
This should not happen in the current version. Each user's test list is filtered by their district. If you see tests from other districts, please report this to your admin.

For additional support, contact your District Administration Office — Tonk, Rajasthan.
