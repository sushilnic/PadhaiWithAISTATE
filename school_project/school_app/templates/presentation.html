01 · Why This Project?

Problem → Solution

Government schools in Tonk district faced real challenges — PadhaiWithAI solves them directly.

 Before PadhaiWithAI

  • No single system to track student marks across schools
  • Manual attendance — time-consuming, error-prone
  • No way to identify weak students early
  • Teachers had no AI help for question creation
  • District officers had no real-time school data
  • Students had no digital learning support in Hindi
  • Paper-based test results — hard to analyse

 After PadhaiWithAI

  • Centralised marks entry & analysis for all schools
  • One-click digital attendance with summaries
  • Automatic weak student detection with alerts
  • AI generates question papers in seconds
  • District dashboard with live school performance
  • AI Tutor (Sathi) answers in Hindi 24/7
  • Visual charts & PDF reports instantly

  Secure Login

Email + password login with CAPTCHA protection. Each user only sees their own data — no cross-district leakage.

When a block officer logs in, they automatically see only their block's schools and students — no manual filtering needed.

  Activity Logs

Every login and action is recorded. District officers can review who accessed what and when — full audit trail.

05 · Powered by AI

AI Learning Tools

Four AI tools built directly into the platform — no separate app needed

🧮 Math Problem Solver

Type or photograph any math problem. Get a full step-by-step solution with explanation — in Hindi or English.

  • Works for Class 6–12 mathematics
  • Nicely formatted equations (LaTeX)
  • Generates similar practice problems
  • Supports photo / image input

🤖 AI Sathi (Smart Tutor)

A 24/7 AI tutor that only answers questions from the student's own subject and chapter — no off-topic distractions.

  • Locked to NCERT curriculum
  • Answers in Hindi for easy understanding
  • Reads answers aloud (text-to-speech)
  • Student can download answers as PDF

📄 Question Paper Generator

Teachers select subject, class, marks, and difficulty. AI creates a full question paper with answer key — in seconds.

  • MCQ, True/False, Fill-in-blank, Short & Long
  • Choose easy / medium / hard mix
  • Print-ready format with PadhaiWithAI branding
  • Answer key generated automatically

📝 AI Practice Tests

Students pick a topic and get a timed AI-generated test. Score is shown instantly with correct answers.

  • Chapter-wise topic selection
  • Auto-scored with feedback
  • Tracks which topics need more practice
  • Progress history saved per student
